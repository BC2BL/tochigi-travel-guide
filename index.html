Jul 13

Arrival: Narita → Utsunomiya

Travel eveningStay: Utsunomiya
Arrival

Arrive Narita Airport at 17:50.

Transfer

Take airport rail toward Tokyo, then connect to the Tohoku Shinkansen for Utsunomiya.

Dinner

Keep dinner simple near Utsunomiya Station, such as gyoza or convenience food if late.

Evening

Check in and sleep. Do not pick up the rental car or drive after the long international flight.

Hotel

Utsunomiya Station area.

Decision note

This avoids jet-lag driving and keeps July 14 fresh for the first rental-car day.

Jul 14

Central Tochigi / Oya

Easy first driveStay: Utsunomiya
Morning

Pick up rental car near Utsunomiya Station. Visit Oya History Museum and Oya Temple.

Lunch

Utsunomiya gyoza or local soba.

Afternoon

Wakayama Farm Bamboo Forest, Ryumon Falls, or Kitsuregawa Onsen depending on energy and weather.

Evening

Return to Utsunomiya for dinner.

Hotel

Utsunomiya.

Decision note

Best first sightseeing day because the driving is short and flexible.

Jul 15

Nikko Heritage

Culture + onsenStay: Nikko/Kinugawa
Morning

Drive to Nikko. Visit Toshogu Shrine, Rinno-ji Temple, and Futarasan Shrine.

Lunch

Nikko yuba cuisine or soba.

Afternoon

Shinkyo Bridge, Tamozawa Imperial Villa, cedar avenue, or historic town walk.

Evening

Check into a Nikko or Kinugawa Onsen ryokan.

Hotel

Nikko or Kinugawa Onsen.

Decision note

This is the one hotel move worth making because Nikko is more atmospheric after day-trippers leave.

Afternoon

Ryuzu Falls, Oku-Nikko viewpoints, then return toward Utsunomiya.

Evening

Dinner in Utsunomiya.

Hotel

Utsunomiya.

Seasonal Planner

Adjust priorities based on month and weather.

Mar-Apr

Best areas: South, Central, Nikko

Prioritize: Ashikaga, Tenpyo no Oka, Ujie Yuyu, Oya

Caution: Crowds around bloom peaks

Adjustment: Make South Tochigi a full day

Late Apr-May

Best areas: South

Prioritize: Ashikaga Flower Park

Caution: Very crowded at peak wisteria

Adjustment: Promote Ashikaga to Tier 1

Jun-Jul

Best areas: Nikko, Nasu

Prioritize: Kirifuri, Lake Chuzenji, Nasu

Caution: Rainy season

Adjustment: Keep rainy-day backup: Oya/Honda

Aug

Best areas: Nasu, Oku-Nikko

Prioritize: Nasu, Lake Chuzenji

Caution: Summer crowds

Adjustment: Start early; onsen evening

Oct-Nov

Best areas: Nikko, Nasu, Shiobara

Prioritize: Irohazaka, Kegon, Momijidani Bridge

Caution: Traffic congestion

Adjustment: Stay in Nikko/Kinugawa to reduce stress

Dec-Feb

Best areas: Kinugawa, Oya, Honda, Ashikaga

Prioritize: Honda Hall, Oya, Ashikaga illumination

Caution: Snow/ice in mountain areas

Adjustment: Avoid high mountain routes if weather is bad
